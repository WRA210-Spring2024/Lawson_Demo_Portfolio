Hi, I'm Vee Lawson.

I'm a multidisciplinary scholar working across rhetoric and writing & feminist and gender studies.

Most of my work concerns ephemeral texts, rhetorical intimacy, and feminist/queer/trans community formation online.
In particular, I draw on cultural rhetorics and digital feminist practices to understand how we can use writing to form supportive communities, especially in times of hardship, under surveillance, and in other adverse environments.
